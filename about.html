More Facts About Me

My name is Oluchukwu Ochulo but I go by Oluchi, the short form of my first name. Oluchi and Oluchukwu mean the same thing in the Igbo language spoken in Nigeria, i.e, "work of God". I am the youngest child in a family of five.

I am 20 years old and a Sophomore at Brigham Young University. I started off majoring in Biochemistry with intent to apply to medical school after obtaining my bachelor's degree. I have since changed my focus to the pre Information Systems courses which I took this semester, hoping to apply to the IS program.

Over the years I have developed a lot of hobbies, all of them having to do with handiwork, creativity, and making something new from nothing or from other materials. I have come to appreciate the common ground I've found between these hobbies and the programming skills I've picked up so far.

Some of these hobbies I have been able to monetize, while some others I still just do for fun. They are;

  1. Monetized
    • Crocheting
    • Hair styling and braiding
  2. Just for fun
    • Drawing
    • Watercolor painting
    • Working on puzzles
